## Design principles
- The player is **not** a frontline hero.
- The game is about **keeping a failing system alive**, not achieving perfect defense.
- Most failures should create **degradation**, not immediate defeat.
- The strongest payoffs come from **smart preparation** and **watching that preparation work later**.
