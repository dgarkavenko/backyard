# Intended Role

## Role statement
The player is not a soldier in command of overwhelming force.
The player is a **reserve-line operator**.

Their identity is built from:
- inspection
- upkeep
- interpretation
- adaptation
- triage
- recovery

## Fantasy of competence
The player expresses skill by:
- noticing weak signals early
- preparing for likely failure
- reshaping defense under scarcity
- deciding what to protect
- surviving bad nights without immediate collapse

## Emotional profile
The role should evoke:
- solitude
- duty
- caution
- ritual
- responsibility
- stubbornness

## What the player is not
The player is not:
- an elite assault unit
- a base-building tycoon
- a horde shooter protagonist
- a commander operating from abstract menus

## Signature self-conceptions
The player should be able to think:
- I know this station
- I can feel when the line is wrong
- I am holding this together by hand
- I can survive a bad night if I do not panic
- I am not winning, but I am not letting it fail yet
