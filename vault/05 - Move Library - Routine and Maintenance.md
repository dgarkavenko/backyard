# Move Library — Routine and Maintenance

## Core idea
These moves establish ritual, loneliness, station familiarity, and maintenance debt.

## Moves

### Do the full perimeter walk
**Why:** re-establish certainty.  
**Accomplishes:** reveals damage, weak points, contamination, or forgotten setup errors.  
**Cost / risk:** consumes prep time.

### Skip the perimeter walk and trust the instruments
**Why:** save time for repairs, crafting, or salvage.  
**Accomplishes:** accelerates preparation.  
**Cost / risk:** increases vulnerability to unseen physical problems.

### Check a single weak point twice
**Why:** suspicion overrules broad coverage.  
**Accomplishes:** greater local confidence.  
**Cost / risk:** the rest of the outpost goes unobserved.

### Perform preventive maintenance on a system that still works
**Why:** avoid future emergency failure.  
**Accomplishes:** lowers breakdown risk later.  
**Cost / risk:** immediate time and part expenditure.

### Leave a damaged system limping
**Why:** something else matters more right now.  
**Accomplishes:** preserves current resources.  
**Cost / risk:** future breakdown pressure increases.

### Replace a part early
**Why:** reliability matters more than extracting full value.  
**Accomplishes:** stabilizes a key system.  
**Cost / risk:** reduced efficiency.

### Cannibalize a noncritical system for parts
**Why:** preserve a more important node.  
**Accomplishes:** immediate repair or upgrade elsewhere.  
**Cost / risk:** creates a blind spot or future debt.

### Recalibrate a monitor cluster
**Why:** improve threat interpretation.  
**Accomplishes:** sharper information quality.  
**Cost / risk:** attention is locked while the world keeps moving.

### Restore a sense of order
**Why:** visible order supports confidence.  
**Accomplishes:** improves player comprehension and planning.  
**Cost / risk:** may not pay off materially right away.

### Work through a checklist
**Why:** ritual reduces panic and oversight.  
**Accomplishes:** reliable coverage of routine tasks.  
**Cost / risk:** can become brittle when novelty appears.

### Break the checklist on instinct
**Why:** something feels wrong.  
**Accomplishes:** enables adaptive play.  
**Cost / risk:** can lead to wasted effort or brilliant saves.

### Stand still and listen to the outpost
**Why:** ambient rhythm is informational.  
**Accomplishes:** reveals machinery stress, distant impacts, or line irregularities.  
**Cost / risk:** no immediate material progress.
